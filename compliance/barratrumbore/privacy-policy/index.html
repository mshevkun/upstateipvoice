SMS Privacy Policy

Barra & Trumbore Stone Fabricators

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Barra & Trumbore Stone Fabricators ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you receive text messages from us or interact with our SMS programs.

Information we collect

When you opt in to receive SMS messages, we may collect and store your mobile phone number, your name (if provided), message content you send to us, and records of messages we send to you. We use this information solely to provide the messaging services described below.

How we use SMS information

We use your mobile number and related information to send you text messages about:

  • appointment and scheduling confirmations
  • technician or installer arrival updates
  • project coordination and status updates
  • customer support and service replies

Sharing of information

We do not sell or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers who help us deliver text messages (such as our telecommunications provider) solely to operate our messaging program on our behalf, under contractual obligations to protect your data.

How you opt in

Customers may opt in verbally or in writing during a phone call, in-person visit, quote, or scheduling conversation. Consent is documented by our staff.

How to opt out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message we send. After you opt out, we will send a one-time confirmation and will not send further marketing or non-essential messages unless you opt in again or we are required to send a transactional message permitted by law.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us using the information below.
