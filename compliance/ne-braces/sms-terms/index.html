Program description

By providing your mobile number and opting in, you agree to receive SMS text messages from W Dental, PLLC. Message types may include:

  • appointment and scheduling confirmations
  • customer support and service replies

Messages may include links to our website or related resources. Messages may include phone numbers you can call.

SMS consent

SMS consent is not required as a condition of purchasing goods or services.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We will not share your mobile information with third parties for marketing.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies depending on your relationship with us and your activity (for example, appointment reminders or service updates). You may receive multiple messages per project or service visit.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details.

Opt out

You can cancel SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You will receive a one-time confirmation message. No further messages will be sent unless you opt in again.
